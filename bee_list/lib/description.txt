U can add a item
Each item will have a title.

U can add a "text box", "list tile" and "notifier" to that item.
Text box will be a simple text box 
with time and date and 
option for delete and edit 
with text box title or only content. 

List tile will have a check box 
a text content and 
a quantity with unit. 

Notifier will have 
a text content to be shown on notification and 
time at which notification will be popup.
